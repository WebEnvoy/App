## Safety Boundary

- App sends a bodyless user-confirmation intent only to `POST /runtime/sessions/{runtime_session_ref}/manual-authentication-completed`.
- The action is fail-closed unless the projection is a Harbor live identity with an active user-held managed session.
- App only displays the returned public identity state and then refetches owner projections; it never infers authentication from browser/page signals or stores sensitive material.
- Recovery may create a session only after a validated Harbor HTTP 404 owner body with `schema_version=harbor-runtime-facts/v0`, the exact cached `runtime_session_ref`, `status=unavailable`, `failure_class=session_missing|session_lost`, public `message`, `retryable=true`, and `current_error.code=session_lost` / `retryable=true`; transport, authorization, server, malformed, mismatched schema/ref, incomplete, or fixture/demo markers remain fail closed.
